Des bases d’électronique

  • S = Speaker View
  • F = Full screen
  • Escape = Zoom Out View
  • Ctrl + Click = Zoom/Unzoom Into a Slide
  • B = (Un)Pause presentation (Black Screen)
  • hjkl = move around
  • n, p = next, previous
